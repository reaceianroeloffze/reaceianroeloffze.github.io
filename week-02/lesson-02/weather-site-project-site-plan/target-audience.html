Nature's Moods Site Plan

Target Audience for Weather Site Project

Weather enthusiasts, event planners, outdoor enthusiasts, news lovers, travelers, travel agents, families planning a vactation, couples going on a honeymoon... Anyone looking for a place to go with great weather and lots of events (maybe?), this website is for all those looking to get out. We are a community-based site which offers news reports, reviews and even a forum where people can find out more about events, the best local spots to hit up, the type of weather and much more. Weather reports will be available and other resources, like wind chill factor, humidity, and best time of the year for events or vacations.
