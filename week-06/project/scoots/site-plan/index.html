Purpose:

Are you the kind of person who likes to do your homework before going on a tour? Are you looking to spend your next tour on the island of Cozumel and want to know what to look forward to? Do you like to be thoroughly informed before going on a tour? If the answer to these questions is "yes", then the new Scoots website is just for you! Aimed at providing as much information as possible about the services offered at Scoots, this website will help you choose rental vehicles to match your travel itinerary as well as provide other helpful information services to help you plan out and maximise your time and experince on the island of Cozumel, such as age restrictions for vehicle use and delivery information. Are you ready to have the time of your life?
