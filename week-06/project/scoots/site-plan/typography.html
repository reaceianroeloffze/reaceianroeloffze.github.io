Scoots Site Plan

Typography for Scoots Site

Heading Font

Italic Poppins, because it compliments the boldness of travel, adventure & exploration as well as the relaxation of a vacation. This font gives one the urge to explore and relax at the same time and to put oneself out there and try something new. It also has the adrenaline packed with riding scooters and renting other vehicles. It has a great vehicle vibe to it.

Paragraph Font

Josefin Sans, because of much of the same reasons as choosing Italic Poppins, but also because it has refinement and formality as extra features, like a waiter dressed in a tuxedo welcoming you into a 5-star silver service restaurant, receiving the best service money can and can't buy. Both these fonts also really compliment each other very well.
