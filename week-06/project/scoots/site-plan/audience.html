Scoots Site Plan

Target Audience for Scoots Project:

The target audience for this site will be young adults between ages 18 & 30.

Personas:

Mike Hollard

Mike Hollard is a 21-year-old undergraduate who loves to take vacations and tours. He saves up each year to be able to go on a holiday/tour at least once. Mike is a part-timer who earns enough money to get by, plus a little extra. As he frequents the net for suitable tourist/holiday destination, this site will prove vital as to whether or not visiting Cozumel will be worth the trouble or not.

Nancy Blaine

Nancy Blaine is a young single adult in her mid-20s who loves to travel. Her favourite spot is the beach. Each Summer, she travels to various costal beaches with her family as her mom and dad travel often for work. She also loves to ride scooters. This site will give her the opportunity to see not just a beach, but a whole island while renting a scooter. An ideal place for a beach bum.

Kevin Hartman

Scenarios:

  • How accurate & reliable will the info provided on this site be?
  • How often is the information and services updated?
  • How often are the prices for rentals and other services updated?
  • How good a dating spot will this be??
  • Is this a fun place to come with friends?
  • What effects will weather conditions have on the services provided??
